Information about cookies

A cookie is a small piece of data sent from a website and stored in a user's web browser or other IT equipment when the user is using the website. It enables the browser to collect information about user behavior but may also be used for security or user functionality. Cookies are used by most websites and is often a requirement for the website to function properly.

Cookies on this site

On this site cookies are used for

User functionality
Caching of user preferences such as last used map type.
Caching of data
Caching of static data (e.g. ports) to lower the amount of data transfered between computer (often on a ship) and the server.
Security
Identification of logged in user as well as preventing unauthorized users to interact with the website

Other websites may use cookies for purposes such as marketing and statistics

Avoiding cookies

If you should wish to not use cookies, then your can do this by configuring your browser settings.

If you should choose to not use cookies completely via your browser setting, then your activity on the Internet will NOT be registered. You will however also prevent MSI-NM as well as many other websites from working properly.

If cookies are not desired and if browser settings allows to do so, then we suggest to set browser settings that only prevent marketing and statistics cookies as well as other third party cookies.

Why are we informing about cookies?

We are as other Danish websites obligated to inform about cookies as stated in Executive Order on Information and Consent Requiring in Case of Storing or Accessing Information in End-User Terminal Equipment (pdf)
